How to Cook Pilau

A simple Kenyan-style Pilau recipe using meat and rich spices.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kg of meat (beef or goat)
  • Onions (3 large, sliced)
  • Pilau masala
  • Pilau whole spices (cardamom, cloves, cinnamon, bay leaf)
  • Curry powder
  • Cumin powder
  • 1 kg of rice (basmati)
  • Salt to taste
  • Cooking oil
  • Water

Steps

  1. Wash and soak the rice for 15â€“20 minutes.
  2. In a sufuria (cooking pot), heat oil and add the sliced onions. Cook until golden brown.
  3. Add the whole spices and stir for a few seconds to release aroma.
  4. Add meat and cook until browned.
  5. Add curry powder, cumin powder, and pilau masala. Mix well.
  6. Add water (about 2 cups for every 1 cup of rice) and let it boil.
  7. Add soaked rice and salt to taste. Stir once.
  8. Cover tightly and let it simmer on low heat until all water is absorbed and rice is cooked.
  9. Turn off the heat, fluff gently with a fork, and serve hot.
